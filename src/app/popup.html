Quick Tips
  • The first line of your note will be used as the title
  • Items can be re-ordered by dragging and dropping to another position in the list
  • Your notes will be automatically saved as Google Documents in the folder '' in your Google Drive
  • You can restore a trashed note from the Google Drive Trash folder
  • You can adjust paragraph margins in the Options
